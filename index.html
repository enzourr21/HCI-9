Enrollment Process

How would you like to enroll?

Your process depends on your student type. Follow the steps that apply to you.

Old / Continuing Students

You have an existing WMSU Student ID and were enrolled in a previous semester.

2nd Year & Above Has Student ID
  • 1
    Log In to the PortalUse your Student ID and password to access your account.
  • 2
    Clear ObligationsSettle any balance or pending concerns with the Registrar, Library, or department.
  • 3
    Confirm SubjectsReview pre-enrolled subjects or manually add subjects as allowed.
  • 4
    Print Assessment FormGenerate your assessment showing tuition, miscellaneous, and other charges.
  • 5
    Pay FeesPay at the University Cashier or via GCash / bank transfer.
  • 6
    Get Your CORDownload your Certificate of Registration after payment is confirmed.

New Students

First time at WMSU? Your process begins with an online application before enrollment proper.

Freshmen Transferee Shiftee
  • 1
    Create an Application AccountRegister with a valid email to access the WMSU Admission Portal.
  • 2
    Submit Application & DocumentsFill up the form and upload required credentials (see Requirements).
  • 3
    Entrance Exam / EvaluationFreshmen take the CET. Transferees/Shiftees undergo credential evaluation.
  • 4
    Receive Admission NoticeCheck your email and portal for your acceptance status and student number.
  • 5
    Enroll & Print AssessmentLog in, confirm subjects, and generate your assessment form.
  • 6
    Pay Fees & Get CORPay at the Cashier or online and download your Certificate of Registration.

Freshmen

Senior high school graduates enrolling in college for the first time at WMSU.

  • Take the WMSU College Admission Test (CET)
  • Pass qualifying grade per college
  • Submit original documents upon enrollment
  • Attend orientation before COR release

Transferee

Students from another school who wish to continue their studies at WMSU.

  • Submit Honorable Dismissal / Transfer Credentials
  • Undergo subject equivalency evaluation
  • Approved by the Dean of the receiving college
  • Comply with residency requirements

Shiftee

Current WMSU students changing their degree program or college within the university.

  • Get shifting form from current department
  • Approval from both current & receiving depts.
  • Subject equivalency assessment
  • Minimum GPA requirement may apply per college
Documentary Requirements

What documents do you need?

Prepare the following before starting enrollment. All documents must be clear, complete, and up-to-date.

Old / Continuing Students

  • WMSU Student ID (or receipt of ID application)
  • Clearance form from previous semester
  • Updated contact information (if changed)
  • Scholarship documents (if applicable)
  • PWD / Solo Parent ID (if applicable for discount)
  • Signed Enrollment Agreement Form

Freshmen

  • Original Senior High School Report Card (Form 138)
  • Original & Photocopy of Birth Certificate (PSA)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • 2 pcs 1×1 and 2 pcs 2×2 ID photos (white bg)
  • CET Exam Result / Admission Slip
  • Accomplished WMSU Application Form
  • Medical Certificate from licensed physician

Transferee

  • Honorable Dismissal from previous school
  • Certified True Copy of Grades (all semesters)
  • Course Syllabus / Subject Descriptions
  • Original Birth Certificate (PSA)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • 2 pcs 1×1 and 2 pcs 2×2 ID photos
  • WMSU Application Form for Transferees

Shiftee

  • Accomplished Shifting Application Form
  • Certified True Copy of Grades (all WMSU semesters)
  • Recommendation from current Department Chair
  • Acceptance from receiving Department Chair
  • Updated WMSU Student ID
  • Clearance from current college / department
