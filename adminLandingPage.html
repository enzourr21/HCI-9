Responsibilities

Admin duties during enrollment

A clear breakdown of tasks each role must complete during the enrollment period.

📝

Admission Office

Pre-enrollment phase
  • Open and manage the online application portal
  • Review and verify submitted documents from applicants
  • Administer and encode CET results
  • Issue admission notices to qualified applicants
  • Coordinate slot allocation with respective deans
  • Manage waitlists and alternative course assignments
🎓

Dean & Department Head

Approval & oversight
  • Set and update subject offerings per semester
  • Approve shiftee applications and transferee acceptance
  • Review and approve student overload requests
  • Monitor enrollment progress within the college
  • Coordinate with advisers on curriculum exceptions
  • Sign off on final enrollment summary reports
👤

Adviser

Student-level guidance
  • Pre-enroll continuing students' subjects before enrollment opens
  • Verify student standing and curriculum compliance
  • Approve or flag subject additions and changes
  • Flag students with academic concerns to the Department Head
  • Assist students in loading/underloading requests
  • Endorse graduating students for final enrollment check
💰

Assessment & Secretary

Financial & documentation
  • Generate and validate student fee assessments
  • Apply scholarship and discount deductions accurately
  • Confirm payment receipts and update payment status
  • Process and release Certificate of Registration (COR)
  • Handle late enrollment surcharge documentation
  • File and archive enrollment records per semester
🔒

Secure Access

All admin sessions are encrypted and require institutional credentials. Two-factor authentication is enabled for sensitive roles.

📋

Audit Logging

Every action performed in the admin portal is timestamped and logged. Logs are accessible only to MISTO and are retained for 5 years.
