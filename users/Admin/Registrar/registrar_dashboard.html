Reg. Rules & Controls

Global enrollment toggles. Changes take effect immediately across all portals.

Enrollment & Course Access
Global Enrollment Lock
Prevents all students from submitting enrollment forms. Use during maintenance or between periods.
Prerequisite Override
Allows advisers to enroll students in subjects without satisfied prerequisites. Requires audit justification.
Capacity Buffer
Allows sections to exceed stated cap by up to 10% before hard-blocking enrollment.
Department Submissions Open
Allows Deans and Secretaries to submit faculty loads and prospectus drafts to the Registrar for review.
Curriculum & Prospectus
Multi-Version Editing
Allows Secretaries to edit a future curriculum version while the current version remains locked.
Publish to College Departments
Releases curriculum to Dean and Secretary of each department. Secretaries may begin building their prospectus once enabled.
Publish to Student Portal
Makes the finalized prospectus visible to students. Must publish to College Departments first.
Requires "Publish to College Departments" enabled first.
Grade & Assessment
Grade Encoding Window
Sets the period during which faculty may submit grades.
Late Grade Submission Lock
Prevents faculty from submitting grades after the encoding window. Registrar must re-open for exceptions.
INC Resolution Period
Separate window for resolving Incomplete grades from previous semester.
Release Grades to Student Portal
Controls whether students can view their finalized grades.
Grade Appeal Window
Opens the formal period where students may contest a grade.
Assessment Office Access
Locks or unlocks the Assessment Office's ability to generate Statements of Account.
Fee Schedule Publish
Controls whether the current semester's fee table is visible to the Assessment Office.
