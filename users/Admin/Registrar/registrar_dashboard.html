    Reg. Rules & Controls
    Global enrollment toggles. Changes take effect immediately across all portals.
    Enrollment & Course Access
    Global Enrollment Lock
    Prevents all students from submitting enrollment forms. Use during system maintenance or between registration periods.
    Prerequisite Override
    Allows advisers to manually enroll students in subjects they have not satisfied prerequisites for. Use sparingly — requires audit justification.
    Capacity Buffer
    Allows sections to exceed their stated cap by up to 10% before hard-blocking enrollment.
    Curriculum & Prospectus
    Multi-Version Editing
    Allows Secretaries to edit a future curriculum version (e.g., planning for next A.Y.) while the current version remains locked for the active semester. Both versions are visible simultaneously.
    Publish to College Departments
    Releases the curriculum to the Dean and Secretary of each department. Secretaries may begin building their prospectus once this is enabled.
    Publish to Student Portal
    Makes the finalized prospectus visible to students. Must publish to College Departments first.
    Requires "Publish to College Departments" to be enabled first.
    Grade & Assessment
    Grade Encoding Window
    Sets the period during which faculty may submit grades. Automatically closes on the end date.
    Late Grade Submission Lock
    Prevents faculty from submitting grades after the encoding window closes, even manually. The Registrar must re-open the window for exceptions.
    INC Resolution Period
    Separate window for resolving Incomplete grades from the previous semester. Independent of the regular grade encoding window.
    Release Grades to Student Portal
    Controls whether students can view their finalized grades. Grades may be internally finalized before this is enabled.
    Grade Appeal Window
    Opens the formal period where students may contest a grade. Closes automatically on end date.
    Assessment Office Access
    Locks or unlocks the Assessment Office's ability to generate Statements of Account. Disable during fee structure finalization.
    Fee Schedule Publish
    Controls whether the current semester's fee table is visible to the Assessment Office. Publish only after the Registrar has finalized the fee structure.
